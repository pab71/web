Jasper

Lyme Regis

Description:

Quietly tucked away in the heart of Lyme Regis, Jasper is a two bedroom holiday property offering comfortable and well equipped accommodation for couples or smaller families. Located just behind the main shopping street in the heart of this unspoilt Dorset seaside town, this is the perfect choice for those seeking a seaside apartment with all the amenities of this attractive town right on the doorstep.

The seafront promenade, shops, restaurants, pubs and gardens are all just a stone's throw from the property.

Jasper has been recently refurbished and benefits from a newly fitted kitchen and bathroom. The open plan living area, with large flat screen TV, enjoys some pleasant views across the town with glimpses to the sea. There is allocated off road parking for one car (see details below) and wifi access is included.

As well as a sandy beach and seafront promenade, Lyme Regis includes celebrity chef Mark Hix's superb Oyster and Seafood Restaurant, along with many other restaurants, cafes, pubs and tearooms. Lyme Regis is a wonderful location for those looking for a traditional seaside break.

There are a wide selection of independent shops, as well as an award winning museum, a small theatre and a cinema. For the more adventurous the South West Coast Path heads west through the amazing Undercliffe between Lyme Regis and Axmouth, or east towards Charmouth and the magnificent National Trust land at Golden Cap.

For those planning a holiday without a car, the mainline railway station in nearby Axminster is served by a regular bus service (or taxis), to and from the town.
